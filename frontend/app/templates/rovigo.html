Rovigo

About Rovigo

Rovigo is a picturesque town situated in the Veneto region of northeastern Italy. Steeped in history and surrounded by the fertile plains of the Po Valley, Rovigo offers a charming blend of architectural beauty, cultural heritage, and a relaxed way of life.


Must-Visit Places

  • Palazzo Roncale
  • Cathedral of Rovigo
  • Vittorio Emanuele II's square

Discover the Beauty

Rovigo's culinary scene is influenced by the bounty of its agricultural surroundings, with local dishes showcasing the flavors of fresh produce and rice, a regional specialty. The town's unhurried pace, vibrant traditions, and welcoming atmosphere make it a delightful destination for those seeking an authentic Italian experience.
