Treviso

About Treviso

Treviso is a charming city in the Veneto region of northeastern Italy. Known for its rich history, artistic heritage, and picturesque landscapes, Treviso offers a unique blend of medieval architecture and modern amenities. The city is traversed by canals and retains a well-preserved medieval city center with narrow cobbled streets and historic buildings.


Must-Visit Places

  • Piazza dei Signori
  • Palazzo dei Trecento
  • Cathedral of San Pietro
  • Church of San Francesco

Discover the Beauty

In addition to its historical and cultural attractions, Treviso is a culinary destination, offering a variety of local dishes and specialties. The city's relaxed atmosphere and proximity to the Venetian lagoon make it an appealing destination for those seeking a blend of history, art, and authentic Italian experiences.
