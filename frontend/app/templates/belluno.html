Belluno

About Belluno

Belluno is a charming town located in the Veneto region of northern Italy, situated in the foothills of the Dolomite Mountains. Nestled within a picturesque valley, Belluno is known for its stunning natural surroundings, historic architecture, and cultural heritage. The town boasts a well-preserved medieval city center with narrow winding streets, charming squares, and colorful buildings.


Must-Visit Places

  • Cattedrale di San Martino
  • Palazzo dei Rettori
  • Civic Tower

Discover the Beauty

Culturally, Belluno has a rich history influenced by both Italian and Austrian traditions. The town has retained its distinct character and serves as a gateway to the Dolomite region, known for its unique blend of alpine and Mediterranean cultures. Belluno's welcoming atmosphere, historical charm, and proximity to the breathtaking Dolomite landscapes make it a delightful destination for those seeking a combination of cultural exploration and outdoor adventure.
